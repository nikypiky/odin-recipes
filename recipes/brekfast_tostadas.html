Black Bean Breakfast Tostadas

These simple black bean breakfast tostadas are fresh, full of flavor, and ready to eat in just minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 each tostada shells
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 medium avocado, peeled and pitted
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 4 slices pepper Jack cheese
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs eggs
  • Â¼ cup salsa
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Step 1 Divide tostada shells between 4 plates.
  2. Step 2 Combine back beans, avocado, and lime juice in a bowl. Gently stir to combine. Divide mixture between tostada shells. Top each tostada with a slice of cheese.
  3. Step 3 Heat oil in a small skillet over medium-high heat. Crack an egg into the hot oil. Cook for 2 minutes. Flip and cook until set, 2 minutes more. Transfer egg to the top of one of the prepared tostada shells. Repeat with remaining eggs.
  4. Step 4 Top each egg with salsa. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
